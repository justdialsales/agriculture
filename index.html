The National Growth Engine

The Indian agricultural equipment sector is on a rapid growth trajectory, fueled by increasing mechanization, rising farm incomes, and strong government support. This presents a massive opportunity for suppliers to expand their reach and sales.

Market Value Projection (Pan-India, INR Billion)

The market is projected to more than double by 2033, driven by a strong CAGR of over 8%. This sustained growth indicates a long-term, stable demand for agricultural machinery.

Equipment Market Share (2024)

While tractors form the backbone of the market, the harvester segment is the fastest-growing category, presenting a key area for diversification and sales focus.

The Rajasthan Opportunity: Subsidies & Demand

With agriculture forming over 26% of its GSVA, Rajasthan is a critical market. Government schemes are actively fueling demand by making modern equipment more affordable for a vast base of farmers.

Government Subsidy Levels by Beneficiary

Subsidies significantly lower the entry barrier for equipment purchase. The high subsidy for CHCs (Custom Hiring Centres) also creates a massive B2B opportunity for bulk sales.
